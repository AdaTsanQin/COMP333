Contract

Responsibilities of Shoppers
  1. Purchase items accurately according to the list of items provided by Buyers. If there is a shortage of stock, Shoppers need to request a replacement from the user.
  2. Submit the items to Buyers within the specified time. If you exceed the time limit, the consumption will be reduced by 30%.
  3. The number of timeouts is positively correlated with the Shoppers' rating. A low rating may result in the platform banning the Shoppers' service for a period of time.
Responsibilities of Buyers
  1. When Shoppers make a replacement request, if Buyers do not respond within two minutes, Shoppers will have the right to choose the replaced item themselves without the approval of Buyers.
  2. After the order is completed, payment needs to be submitted within half an hour. The number of overdue submission penalties is negatively correlated with the Buyers' rating. Too low a rating will result in a longer waiting time for Shoppers to accept Buyers' order.
  3. When making a request, the default tip is a percentage of the total price of the items that Shoppers bring back, rather than a percentage of the total price of the items proposed by Buyers.
Responsibilities for all Users
  1. Payment must be made through the Campus Dash platform.
  2. The platform keeps the funds in escrow until the errand is completed.
The above items are the first draft of the formal contract. The contract in the App may be changed.
