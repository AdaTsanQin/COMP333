How Our App Works

Step 1: Submit a Request

List your items, pick a delivery speed (Turbo or Chill), and set a drop-off spot (Dorm, Classroom).

Step 2: Wait for a Shopper

A "Wes dasher" sees your request, shops for you, and updates you along the way.

Step 3: Arrange Drop-off

Meet at the chosen location, check your items, then leave a rating or tip if you're happy!
